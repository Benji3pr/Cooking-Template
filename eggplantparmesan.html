Eggplant Parmesan

Step 1: Cut each eggplant into about ½-inch (1-cm) thick round slices.

Step 2: Arrange the slices in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with paper towels.

Step 3: Sprinkle the eggplant with half of the coarse salt. Flip the slices over and sprinkle with the rest of the salt.

Step 4: Put another baking sheet on top of the eggplant and place something heavy, like a cast-iron skillet, on the top baking sheet to help release moisture from the eggplant. Let sit for 30 minutes.

Step 5: Uncover the eggplant slices and pat dry with paper towels. Brush most of the salt from the eggplant with a paper towel, then transfer to a medium bowl. Set aside.

Step 6: In a separate medium bowl, combine the flour, oregano, basil, garlic powder, and pepper. Mix well.

Step 7: Heat the vegetable oil in a large frying pan until it reaches 375°F (190°C).

Step 8: Remove the pizza from the oven and place on a cutting board. Let pizza rest for 1-2 Coat each eggplant slice in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess flour.you cut it. Repeat process with remaining dough.

Step 9: Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).

Step 10: Fry the eggplant in batches until golden brown on both sides, 2-3 minutes per side. Drain on a paper towel-lined baking sheet and pat dry using more paper towels to absorb excess oil.

Step 11: Pour about 1 cup (260 g) of marinara sauce into a 2½-quart (2 L) baking dish and spread evenly to cover the bottom of the dish.

Step 12: Add about 8 eggplant slices (it’s okay if they overlap) and cover with ½ cup (130 g) of marinara. Sprinkle with a third of the fresh basil, then top with about a third of the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan. Repeat to make 2 more layers. Finish with the fresh mozzarella cheese and a final sprinkling of Parmesan.

Step 13: Bake for 30 minutes, until the mozzarella is bubbling and golden brown.

Step 14: Let cool for 10 minutes, then serve garnished with fresh basil.

Step 15: Serve and Enjoy!

    Ingredients:
  • 2 medium eggplants, washed
  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (125 g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 /2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (480 mL)
  • 2 1/2 cups marinara sauce, divided (650 g)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, minced, plus more for garnish
  • 2 cups low-moisture mozzarella, shredded (200 g)
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese (100 g)
  • 8 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
