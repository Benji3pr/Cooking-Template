Fried Eggs

Step 1: For Over-Easy or Over-Hard Eggs: Heat 2 tsp. butter in nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot.

Step 2: Break eggs and Slip into pan, 1 at a time, immediately reduce heat to low.

Step 3: Cook SLOWLY until whites are completely set and yolks begin to thicken but are not hard.

Step 4: Slide turner under each egg and carefully Flip it over in pan.

Step 5: Cook second side to desired doneness.

Step 6: Sprinkle with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

    Ingredients:
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
