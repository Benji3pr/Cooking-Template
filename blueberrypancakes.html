Blueberry Pancakes

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar.

Step 2: In a small bowl, beat together egg and milk.

Step 3: Stir milk and egg into flour mixture.

Step 4: Mix in the butter and fold in the blueberries.

Step 5: Set aside for 1 hour.

Step 6: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.

Step 7: Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

Step 8: Brown on both sides and serve hot.

    Ingredients:
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purposed flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 tablespoom butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries, thawed
  • 1 1/4 teaspons white sugar
