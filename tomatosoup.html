Tomato Soup

Step 1: In a stockpot, over medium heat, combine the tomatoes, onion, cloves and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, and gently boil for about 20 minutes to blend all of the flavors.

Step 2: Remove from heat and run the mixture through a food mill into a large bowl, or pan. Discard any stuff left over in the food mill.

Step 3: In the now empty stockpot, melt the butter over medium heat.

Step 4: Stir in the flour to make a roux, cooking until the roux is a medium brown.

Step 5: Gradually whisk in a bit of the tomato mixture, so that no lumps form, then stir in the rest.

Step 6: Season with sugar and salt, and adjust to taste.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy!

    Ingredients:
  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 slice onion
  • 4 whole garlic cloves
  • 2 cups of chiken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
