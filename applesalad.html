Apple Salad

Step 1: In a small bowl, mix lemon juice, avocado oil, vinegar, dijan, basil, and garlic. Whisk and allow to sit.

Step 2: In a large salad bowl, add all salad ingredients.

Step 3: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat. Allow to sit for 10 minutes for flavors to blend.

Step 4: Serve and Enjoy!

    Ingredients:
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch kale, stems removed and massaged
  • 2 apples, chopped
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped basil
